# ResearchStarter
A free browser-based research intelligence tool for graduate students. Paste your research question, get database search strings, key scholars, theoretical frameworks, source scoring, and citation generation. No API key, no setup.


What it does

Generates Boolean search strings for 7 major academic databases (JSTOR, ProQuest, EBSCOhost, Web of Science, Google Scholar, SSRN, and leading think tanks) formatted in each database's exact syntax
Maps key scholars and theoretical frameworks specific to your research question with direct Google Scholar links
Scores sources for credibility and relevance, flags gaps, and generates citations in APA, Chicago, MLA, and Harvard
Grades annotated bibliographies and recommends Keep, Expand, or Replace for each source
Tracks research progress with a focus/done system and filter view
Exports everything to CSV and Zotero RIS

What it doesn't do
It doesn't write your argument, do your analysis, or replace critical thinking. It gets you to the starting line faster so you can focus on the work that actually requires your brain.
How to use it

Open the tool alongside claude.ai
Paste your research question and click Generate Research Package
Copy the prompt into Claude and wait for the response
Paste Claude's response back into the tool
Browse your database strings, authors, and frameworks
Paste sources into the Source Analyzer or Annotated Bibliography tab for scoring

Requirements

A free or Pro Claude.ai account
A modern browser
That's it
